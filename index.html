Welcome to the Global Diary! This is a website that we've designed in our multimedia programming course at California State University, Monterey Bay. As two students whom have studied abroad in our time in college, we've built this site to allow people to connect from around the globe by creating diary entries with a picture from your area or related to your diary post to share with the world!
1) Type your name (leave blank and Anonymous will be placed if you do not wish to expose your identity).
2) Type out a well formed diary post, be as personal or private as you wish!
3) Select a picture. (JPG / JPEG)
4) Make sure you are using a browser with access to your current location.
5) Submit!
Upvotes and Downvotes can be given accordingly, a score of -20 will remove your diary entry so please be mindful of the pictures and content you post.
